About StrayConnected

StrayConnected is a community-driven platform dedicated to helping stray animals in your locality. Whether it's a hungry puppy, an injured dog, or an abandoned cat, our goal is to connect kind-hearted individuals who are ready to help with food, medical care, or even adoption.

You can easily report strays by uploading a photo and their location. Nearby volunteers can then reach out, donate, or arrange veterinary assistance. Every small step brings hope to these voiceless souls.
